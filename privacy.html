Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how SUCSU Election 2026 ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with this election results display website.

1. Information We Collect

This is a static website that displays publicly available election results. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors. Specifically:

  • We do not require user registration or login
  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not track your browsing behavior

2. Information Displayed

The website displays publicly available information about election candidates, including:

  • Candidate names
  • Department and session information
  • Panel affiliations
  • Vote counts

This information is publicly available as part of the SUCSU election process at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology.

3. Third-Party Services

This website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel: For hosting (subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy)
  • Lucide Icons: For interface icons

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information at risk. The website is served over HTTPS to ensure secure transmission of publicly displayed content.

5. Children's Privacy

This website does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
